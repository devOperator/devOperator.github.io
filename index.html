    • Energy

    Everybody consumes it yet there are very few large players in this industry. With the frequent crashes in oil markets every decade or so this is one area where the true value investor can shine. High dividend yields are the norm here as growth is limited. Even for those seeking high growth opportunities the cash flow offered can be hard to pass up.

    Consumer Packaged Goods

    Strong brand names and in many cases high margins make this arena great for building generational wealth. The names your mother used in the kitchen are likely in your kitchen as well. These brands stick and so do the profits.

    Pharmaceuticals

    The margins here are staggering and the market is growing as the population increases in size and in age. This market has traditionally been high on the list for the durability of it's earnings as well as for it's high ROE.

    Data Companies

    Here is a new one. To make the case for the big data industry just look at the user count for some of the giants in the social media space. The amount of data collected by these companies is invaluable to corporations especially as tv subscriptions dwindle. They have to spend those ad dollars somewhere. Some sources have been significantly undervalued and showing great upside potential.
